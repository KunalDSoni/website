About Me

Crafting fintech experiences
with purpose & precision

Payments

I'm a fintech product leader based in the UAE, passionate about building digital financial products that drive inclusion and simplify money management. I've had the privilege of shaping products across payments, neobanking, and payroll solutions at companies like Astratech/Botim, Dopay, Mintok, and Novo.

My approach combines deep fintech domain expertise with data-driven decision making and genuine empathy for users. I believe the best financial products aren't just functional — they're trustworthy, accessible, and empower people to take control of their finances.

When I'm not shipping features, you'll find me exploring the latest in embedded finance, open banking, and AI-driven financial services across the MENA region and beyond.
